PROJECT: ElevateU Website Design System

DESIGN INTENT
- Minimal
- Editorial
- Calm
- High-signal
- Vibe: Notion × Linear × serious ed-tech startup
- Rule: If it doesn’t improve clarity, remove it

GLOBAL PRINCIPLES
- Whitespace over color
- Typography over graphics
- One primary accent color per viewport
- Must be scannable in 5 seconds
- Zero decorative noise

LAYOUT SYSTEM

Desktop
- Max width: 1200px
- Content width: 1040px
- Side padding: 32px
- Vertical spacing: 96px

Mobile
- Width: 100%
- Side padding: 16px
- Vertical spacing: 56px

BREAKPOINTS
Mobile: min-width 0px
Tablet: min-width 768px
Desktop: min-width 1024px

CONTACT

- Email or simple form
- No decorative UI

FOOTER

- Background: bg-muted
- Padding: 32px
- Text size: 14px
- Muted color
- Logo, copyright, links

DO NOT USE

- Background images
- Illustrations
- Gradients
- Shadows
- Stock photos
- Heavy animations
- Glass / neumorphism

FINAL CHECK

- Looks good without images
- Reads like a Notion page
- Calm and serious
- Trust-first, not flashy
